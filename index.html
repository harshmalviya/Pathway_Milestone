ABOUT

Pathway, the placement cell of Ram Lal Anand College makes the ends meet by helping its students get placed into their dream companies. Pathways is comprised of assiduous and diligent scholars and teachers who go the extra mile to ensure the success of the right candidates. As our name suggests, we aim towards building avenues for students to find their dream roles. Not only do we show students the path, but we are also concerned about walking them through it. While regular seminars and workshops prepare students for interview processes, annual internship and placement drives provide them with the opportunities to put these skills to use and get into top companies.
