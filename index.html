ABOUT

Pathway - The Placement Cell of Ram Lal Anand College welcomes you to 'MILESTONE-2022' - its annual internship fair. Scheduled to be held on the 11th and 12th of April, Milestone-2022 is firmly set to aid you to convert the interviews of your dream companies. Milestone 2022 is a journey of miles and Pathway, comprising of assiduous and diligent scholars and mentors is perpetually ensuring the success of every candidate in this journey. As our name suggests, we aim towards building avenues for students to find their dream roles. Not only do we show students the path, but we are also concerned about walking them through it. While regular seminars and workshops prepare students for interview processes, annual internship and placement drives provide them with the opportunities to put these skills to use and get into top companies. The fair is open to all students of the University of Delhi.
